News Archive

gvSIG Fonsagua official release September 23, 2010

gvSIG Fonsagua has been finally officially released. Code repositories and unofficial binaries have been available since some months ago, but we have been waiting for the bugs and the feature request provided by the technicians before make it official. After some improvements gvSIG Fonsagua 0.2 is released as stable. The source code and binaries of this version are available from the download section of the web of the project.

We want to announce also, that Cartolab is going to take part in the VI gvSIG conferences. The main developers of the application are going to be available during the whole congress to ask questions. Also, Francisco Puga is going to present “gvSIG Fonsagua-Information System for management water and sanitation” in the first day of the conferences.

Video tutorials uploaded September 23, 2010

Three video tutorials have been uploaded to the how to section of the web. The tutorials show how to enter information in the application by means of personalized forms, the design of a pumping system and the design a distribution network.

More tutorials will be uploaded in the next weeks. Feel free to send us your own tutorial or suggestion about what aspect of the application should be covered.

gvSIG Fonsagua webpage September 6, 2010

From today on, it is available to the public the brand new gvSIG Fonsagua webpage. During next weeks both web contents and look&feel are going to be improved.

The site intends to become the main source of information for the application. Other information and feedback could be found by using the mailinglist of the project.

gvSIG Fonsagua in FOSS4G September 6, 2010

During this week, the 2010 FOSS4G congress is being held in Barcelona. CartoLab is going to FOSS4G to show the application to all people interested. Pablo Sanxiao and Andrés Maneiro, two developers of gvSIG Fonsagua, are going to be available during the whole congress to ask questions. Also, a specified session to public introduce the posters is being planned by organization. CartoLab is going to present the poster “gvSIG Fonsagua-”Information System for water and sanitation programs” Check congress timetables and places for more information.

2nd training workshops September 6, 2010

During July 2010, a week workshop has been developed in Honduras by Carmen Molejón. The workshop was open to all the technicians in the local NGOs who are going to use the application.

In those workshops, the users have used a beta version of gvSIG Fonsagua with all the functionalities. After the sessions, feedback has been collected to improve the application and solve some minor issues.

Public presentations September 6, 2010

The application gvSIG Fonsagua has been introduced to general and specialized public during 2010, getting some real good feedback. The program and the application has been announced in:

  • A Coruña, May 2010. Cooperation for Development from University. «The case of gvSIG Fonsagua».
  • Valencia, December 2009. V gvSIG International Conference. «Developments using gvSIG to improve the information management in a International Cooperation Project held in Honduras, by Enxeñería Sen Fronteiras»
  • Girona, March 2009. III Free Software GIS Conferences. «Honduras: GIS, Free Software and Human Development»

First training workshop September 6, 2010

During February 2010, the training workshops to technicians have been taught in San Lorenzo (Honduras) by Nacho Varela. The sessions were about a general introduction to GIS, by using gvSIG Fonsagua to show how it works. In this phase, the technicians had already available the GPS importation module and a set of personalized forms to introduce the data. The feedback gathered is being taken into account to develop next phases of application.
