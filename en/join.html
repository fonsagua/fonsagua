Join us

There are a bunch of ways to engage with the project and keep in touch with what is being done. Learn how to participate:

  • Download the application and test it by yourself.
  • Keep update with the last news.
  • Report any errors you have found or send us new proposals through the github issues or email.
  • Get the source code and solve a bug, add a new functionality or adapt the software to your needs. In this case, maybe could be useful to you our roadmap.

We are looking forward to make gvSIG Fonsagua the greatest tool to manage water and sanitation programs. Any feedback you want to share with us will be welcome.
